Quick hummus recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 can (400g) of peas (garbanzo beans)
  • 175g of tahini
  • 6 sundried tomatoes
  • Half a red pepper
  • A pinch of cayenne pepper
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • A dash of olive oil
