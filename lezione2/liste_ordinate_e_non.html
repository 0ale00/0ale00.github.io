Quick hummus recipe

Ingredients

  1. 1 can (400g) of peas (garbanzo beans)
  2. 175g of tahini
  3. 6 sundried tomatoes
  4. Half a red pepper
  5. A pinch of cayenne pepper
  6. 1 clove of garlic
  7. A dash of olive oil

I colori

Colori primari
Rosso, verde, blu
Colori secondari
Giallo + Rosso = Arancione
Giallo + Blu = Verde
Rosso + Blu = Viola
